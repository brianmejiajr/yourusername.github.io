01 — Story

The arc
behind
the work

"I didn't just enroll people in health plans — I learned what breaks inside them. Now I build the data systems that fix it."

I'm a Dominican-American healthcare professional with direct experience inside the health plan enrollment pipeline — guiding members through ACA coverage, navigating prescription assistance programs, and maintaining compliance in environments where a single data error meant someone lost access to care.

That frontline exposure gave me something rare: I understand claims data, member adherence, and cost-of-care gaps from the inside out — not just from a spreadsheet. I've seen where KPIs fail to capture the real story, and where dashboard decisions ripple down to real patients.

Now I'm in Barcelona, pursuing a Master's in Digital Healthcare at the Universitat de Barcelona — deepening my expertise in population health analytics, AI-driven care models, and data strategy to support health systems in reducing unplanned admissions and improving financial performance.
